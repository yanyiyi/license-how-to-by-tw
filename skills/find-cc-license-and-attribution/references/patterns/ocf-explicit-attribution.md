# OCF-Style Explicit Attribution Pattern

## Use When

Use this pattern when a page, report, slide deck, project page, or publication provides exact wording for attribution or asks users to credit a specific person, organization, project, funder, or collaborator.

## Main Risk

Do not replace a requested attribution sentence with only the author's name. CC attribution may include reasonable licensor-designated credit fields beyond the creator.

## Check

1. Search for `請標示`, `使用時請標示`, `引用請註明`, `attribution`, `credit`, `cite`, and `acknowledgement`.
2. Identify whether the requested credit applies to the whole work, a section, an image, a dataset, or an event/publication page.
3. Preserve requested roles such as author, editor, funder, collaborator, publisher, project name, and organization.
4. Check whether embedded logos, partner marks, screenshots, or third-party images are excluded.
5. If the work is a mixed-source document, recommend a full source list or appendix.

## Output Rows

| 使用對象 | Attribution / 應標示對象 | CC 授權名稱 |
|---|---|---|
| **頁面文字／主要作品內容** | 依頁面指定 attribution 文字完整標示 | 依頁面授權正規化 |
| **第三方圖片、Logo 或截圖** | 依各素材旁標示或來源頁面標示 | 若未納入整體授權，填 `非 CC：不在授權範圍內` |

## Notes

For slides, posters, social cards, and exhibition labels, use short visible attribution plus a linked full source list when space is limited.
